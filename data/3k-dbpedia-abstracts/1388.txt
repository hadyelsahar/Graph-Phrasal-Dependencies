David Myers collectively known as the Hairy Bikers, are British television presenters who have fronted the series The Hairy Bikers' Cookbook, The Hairy Bikers Ride Again, The Hairy Bakers, The Hairy Bikers' Food Tour of Britain, The Hairy Bikers Mums Know Best, Hairy Bikers Meals on Wheels, Hairy Biker's Best of British, The Hairy Biker's Bakeation, Hairy Dieters: How to Love Food and Lose Weight and The Hairy Bikers' Asian Adventure for BBC Two, and The Hairy Biker's Mississippi Adventure for Good Food .
